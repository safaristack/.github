-- Principles & Finances --

Safari Stack operates on a "Community Cloud" model. Our primary goal is to aggregate local demand to unlock enterprise-grade peering and hardware for independent developers.

We maintain a transparent 15% margin above operational costs (transit, power, and rack space). These funds are strictly reserved for hardware refreshes, maintenance, and peering expansion.
